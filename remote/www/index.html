PyLadies Remote Organizer

Anna Ossowski

Anna is an aspiring developer from Germany. She holds a degree in English and theology but fell in love with programming a while ago and is now working on building a career in the field.

She is very involved in Django Girls. She co-organized the Django Girls Budapest and PyCon workshops and runs the "Your Django Story" interview series on the Django Girls blog.

Anna is also a member of the Django Software Foundation grants committee, and recently became a director of the Python Software Foundation.

She is very passionate about diversity and community outreach and wants to encourage more women to learn programming because it's awesome! In her free time she writes a blog. You can follow Anna on Twitter at @ossanna16.
